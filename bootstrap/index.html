MINI. JUST NOT AS YOU KNOW IT.

The word "mini" was named after the Latin word "minium", and was later widely used as a modifier for small and cute things when British fashion designer Mary Quant used the name "miniskirt", her favorite car. In other words, the etymology of the miniskirt is Mini, the car. It is characterized by its cute appearance and very small size in reality, but the current model after BMW's acquisition is quite bigger than expected, compared to the early classical mini.

Since it officially entered BMW Korea in 2005, it is an imported car that is often sold to women thanks to its small, cute, and retro sensibility, but it is never an easy item to drive when driven.The suspension is really hard, so the ride is very hard, and the run flat tire comes out pure, so it's a unique imported car that's notorious for not buying if you want a comfortable and small car. It is also displayed in the BMW Driving Center, and you can also experience circuits. Some showrooms/service centers are with BMW.
